### What's the difference between Traditional and AI Analysis?

**Traditional Analysis:**
- 100+ financial metrics
- Fundamental analysis focus
- Altman Z-Score, Piotroski F-Score
- Beneish M-Score
- Technical indicators
- Best for long-term investing

**AI Analysis:**
- 56+ advanced metrics
- Machine learning predictions
- Confidence scoring
- Pattern recognition
- Multiple timeframe predictions
- Best for data-driven decisions

[Learn More](../features/stock-analysis)

### How accurate are the AI predictions?

Our AI models are trained on historical data and continuously improved. While we strive for high accuracy, no prediction model is 100% accurate. Always use AI insights as one of many tools in your investment decision process.

Historical accuracy metrics are displayed with each prediction.

### What is the AI Confidence Score?

The confidence score (0-100%) indicates how confident our AI model is in its prediction:
- **90-100%**: Very High Confidence
- **70-89%**: High Confidence
- **50-69%**: Moderate Confidence
- **30-49%**: Low Confidence
- **0-29%**: Very Low Confidence

### How often is data updated?

- **Stock prices**: Real-time during market hours
- **Analysis results**: Updated daily after market close
- **Financial data**: Updated quarterly (earnings releases)
- **News**: Continuously updated
- **AI models**: Retrained weekly

## Portfolio Management

### How do I add stocks to my portfolio?

**Method 1: Upload File**
1. Go to [Upload Portfolio](https://www.rightstockai.com/portfolio/upload)
2. Download sample format
3. Fill in your holdings
4. Upload file

**Method 2: Manual Entry**
1. Go to [My Portfolios](https://www.rightstockai.com/portfolio)
2. Create new portfolio
3. Add stocks manually
4. Enter purchase details

### Which broker formats are supported?

We support:
- Zerodha holdings
- Groww portfolio
- Upstox statements
- Angel One holdings
- ICICI Direct
- HDFC Securities
- Generic CSV format

### How is portfolio performance calculated?

We calculate:
- **Absolute Returns**: (Current Value - Invested Amount) / Invested Amount
- **XIRR**: Time-weighted returns considering cash flows
- **Benchmark Comparison**: Against NIFTY 50 or Sensex
- **Risk-Adjusted Returns**: Sharpe ratio and Sortino ratio

### Can I track multiple portfolios?

Yes, Pro subscribers can create unlimited portfolios for different strategies or family members.

## Technical Issues

### The website is loading slowly

Try these steps:
1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Check your internet connection
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Try incognito/private mode

Still slow? Contact support@rightstockai.com

### Charts are not loading

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Enable JavaScript
3. Allow third-party cookies
4. Update your browser
5. Try different browser
6. Clear cache

### I can't log in

**Common fixes:**
1. Verify correct email/password
2. Check CAPS LOCK
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try password reset
5. Disable browser extensions
6. Try different browser

### Portfolio upload failed

**Troubleshooting:**
1. Check file format (CSV/Excel)
2. Verify column headers match sample
3. Remove special characters
4. Ensure file size < 5MB
5. Try sample format first
6. Contact support with file

### Analysis is not generating

**Possible causes:**
1. Stock symbol incorrect
2. Market closed (check time)
3. Data not available for stock
4. Server maintenance
5. Subscription expired

Refresh page and try again. If issue persists, contact support.

## Investment Questions

### Is this investment advice?

No, RightStockAI provides analysis tools and insights. We are not registered investment advisors. Always consult a certified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

### Should I invest based solely on AI recommendations?

No, use AI insights as one of several tools. Always:
- Do your own research
- Consider your risk tolerance
- Diversify your portfolio
- Consult financial advisors
- Understand what you're investing in

### What is the minimum investment?

RightStockAI is an analysis platform, not a broker. Investment minimums depend on your broker and the stocks you choose. Most Indian stocks have no minimum beyond 1 share.

### Can you help me choose stocks?

We provide analysis and insights, but cannot provide personalized investment advice. Use our tools to research and make informed decisions.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### "Session Expired" Error
**Solution:** Log out and log back in. Clear cookies if issue persists.

#### Charts Not Updating
**Solution:** Refresh page. Ensure market is open or wait for end-of-day updates.

#### Portfolio Values Incorrect
**Solution:** Verify purchase prices and dates. Check for corporate actions (splits, bonuses).

#### Analysis Takes Too Long
**Solution:** Complex analysis may take 30-60 seconds. If longer, refresh and try again.

#### Payment Failed
**Solution:** Check card details, ensure sufficient balance, try different payment method, or contact your bank.
