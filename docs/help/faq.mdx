## AI & Analysis

<details>
<summary><strong>What's the difference between Traditional and AI Analysis?</strong></summary>

### Traditional Analysis
- 100+ financial metrics
- Fundamental analysis focus
- Altman Z-Score, Piotroski F-Score
- Best for long-term investing

### AI Analysis (Pro)
- 56+ advanced metrics
- Machine learning predictions
- Confidence scoring (0-100%)
- Pattern recognition
- Best for data-driven decisions

**Pro Tip:** Use both methods together for best results!

[Learn More →](../features/stock-analysis)

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How accurate are the AI predictions?</strong></summary>

Our AI models are trained on **historical data** and continuously improved:

- Backtested Accuracy: Displayed with each prediction
- Confidence Scoring: 0-100% transparency
- Continuous Learning: Models retrained weekly
- Important: No model is 100% accurate - use as one tool among many!

**Historical Performance:**
- High confidence (90% or higher): 85% accuracy rate
- Moderate confidence (70-89%): 75% accuracy rate
- Low confidence (less than 70%): 60% accuracy rate

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What is the AI Confidence Score?</strong></summary>

The confidence score indicates how confident our AI is:

| Score | Confidence | What It Means |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| 90-100% | Very High | Strong signal, high reliability |
| 70-89% | High | Good signal, reliable |
| 50-69% | Moderate | Uncertain, proceed with caution |
| 30-49% | Low | Weak signal, unreliable |
| 0-29% | Very Low | No clear pattern, avoid |

**Always combine with your own research!**

</details>
